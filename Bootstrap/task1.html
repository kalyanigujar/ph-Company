Effort and Willpower:

Krishna's teachings emphasize that individuals have the power to shape their own destiny through their thoughts and actions.

Acceptance of Change:

Krishna acknowledges that change is inevitable and encourages acceptance of both good and bad experiences.

Respect and Understanding:

Krishna advises giving others the benefit of the doubt and understanding that people may act out of ignorance rather than malice.

Living in the Present:

Krishna's philosophy encourages living in the present moment and making the most of each experience.
